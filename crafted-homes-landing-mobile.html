Why Somerset

A simpler, faster way to build at Alys—without compromising design, craftsmanship, or legacy.

  • Construction‑ready plans from architects trusted by Alys Beach.
  • Personalize interiors at the Resource Center with curated fixtures & finishes.
  • Seamless coordination across Sales, Construction & Design.
  • Weekly photo updates and milestone communications during the build.
  • On‑site warranty team and dedicated homeowner support.

How It Works

  1. Select your Somerset plan & homesite with an Alys Beach Sales Associate.
  2. Make finish selections with our Owner Liaison at the Resource Center.
  3. Pre‑construction meeting with your Builder Representative.
  4. Receive weekly photo updates and schedule key walk‑throughs.
  5. Orientation, closing & keys—then enjoy your new home.
